Ingredient Substitutions

Most ingredients can be found at any local Asian grocery store. However, below is a comprehensive guide that may help users locate ingredients online if they encounter difficulties along with ingredient substitutions.

Ingredients Guide

Mango Sago

  • Sago: May be substituted for other tapioca pearls such as boba. However, sago is available for purchase on Amazon or Instacart.
  • Coconut milk: May be substituted with any milk (whole milk, half and half, almond milk, etc.).

Hong Kong Egg Tart

  • Cake Flour: Can be made at home using regular all-purpose flour.
    • Cake Flour Recipe (yields 1 cup):
      • Add 1 cup of all-purpose flour to a bowl. Remove 2 tbps of flour and replace with cornstarch. Sift flour at least 5 times. Use within recipe as instructed.
  • Egg Tart Molds: May be substituted by spraying cupcake pans with non-stick spray and mold dough into each cup. However, these molds are available for purchase on Amazon.

Egg Puffs

  • Tapioca starch: May be substituted with rice flour, cornstarch, or cassava flour. However, tapioca starch is available for purchase on Amazon.
  • Egg Puff Machine: Cannot be substituted. However, machine is available for purchase on Amazon.
